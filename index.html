About the College

Memari College is a well-known college in Memari, West Bengal-713146, India. It was established in 1981 and is affiliated with the University of Burdwan. The college offers undergraduate courses in Arts, Science, and Commerce. The college has a library, computer lab, and sports facilities for students. The college also has a placement cell that helps students find jobs after graduation. The faculty at Memari College is highly qualified and experienced. The college has a good reputation for providing quality education to students.
